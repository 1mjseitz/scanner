Cross bar = how many bars ago the 20MA crossed above the 50MA (0 = current bar). A green PASS row means all five conditions are met. "Cross↑" requires the 20MA to be above the 50MA now and below it at the start of the lookback window — a fresh cross, not an old one.
Dist 20MA colors (how far price is from the 20MA, above or below): green = 0–5% (normal) · dark orange = 5–10% (stretched) · red = over 10% (extreme, marked with "!"). Extreme readings often precede a snap-back toward the average.
